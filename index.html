01. Background

A bit about me.

I've been working in information security and GRC for over 15 years. Currently, I'm an Information Security Compliance Manager; before that, I spent most of my career as a consultant.

My consulting years took me through Booz Allen Hamilton, Ernst & Young, Protiviti, Coalfire, Optiv, Schellman, and Online Business Systems. I've worked with Fortune 100 companies and federal agencies on everything from security assessments to technical testing across cloud and on-prem environments.

Outside of the day job, I'm a shameless tinkerer. I run a self-hosted home lab on Proxmox, automate whatever I can, and firmly believe that owning your infrastructure is a right, not a luxury. If it can be scripted, it should be. Privacy is a human right. Encryption is not a crime. Key disclosure laws are unethical.
