01. about

A bit about me

Hey, I'm Ben Mingle, an Information Security Compliance Manager with over 15 years in GRC and security risk. Previously a consultant, I advised C-Suite executives and led engagements across a broad range of regulatory compliance and security risk initiatives at firms ranging from Fortune 100 companies to federal agencies.

My career spans Booz Allen Hamilton, Ernst & Young, Protiviti, Coalfire, Optiv Security, Schellman, and Online Business Systems, building deep hands-on experience in security assessments, compliance program management, and technical configuration testing across on-premises, cloud, and hybrid environments.

Outside of work, I'm an unrepentant tinkerer. I run a self-hosted home lab on Proxmox, automate whatever I can, and believe that owning your infrastructure is a right, not a luxury. If it can be scripted, it should be. Privacy is a human right. Encryption is not a crime. Key disclosure laws are unethical.
