01. about

A bit about me

Hey, I'm Ben Mingle. I've been working in information security and GRC for over 15 years, long enough to have seen a lot of frameworks come and go, and to have strong opinions about most of them. Currently I'm an Information Security Compliance Manager; before that, a consultant for the better part of my career.

Those consulting years took me through Booz Allen Hamilton, Ernst & Young, Protiviti, Coalfire, Optiv, Schellman, and Online Business Systems, a mix of Big 4 advisory, boutique firms, and everything in between. Clients ranged from Fortune 100 companies to federal agencies, and the work covered security assessments, compliance programs, and technical testing across on-prem, cloud, and hybrid environments.

Outside of work, I'm an unrepentant tinkerer. I run a self-hosted home lab on Proxmox, automate whatever I can, and believe that owning your infrastructure is a right, not a luxury. If it can be scripted, it should be. Privacy is a human right. Encryption is not a crime. Key disclosure laws are unethical.
