A bit about me

Hey, I'm Ben Mingle. Currently Information Security Compliance Manager at EchoStar. Before moving into management, I spent a decade as a QSA and consultant leading assessments across the "Big 4" and specialized firms like Coalfire and Schellman.

My expertise spans PCI DSS, NIST, and SOC 2 frameworks. I've built compliance programs for Fortune 100 companies and navigated security challenges across cloud and hybrid environments.

Outside of GRC, I'm into immutable Linux (Bazzite/Fedora Atomic) and run a high-availability home lab on Proxmox. I believe technical sovereignty and privacy are fundamental—encryption is not a crime.
